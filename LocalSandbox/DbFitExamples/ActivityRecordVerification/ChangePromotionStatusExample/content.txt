!3 Get the CHANGE PROMOTION STATUS activities, with ACTMISC broken out by the SQL query.
We are querying by TRANSNBR and TRANSTYPE, both of which are at the 
front of the activity record.  We could also query on ACCTNBR and TRANSTYPE.
!4 If a query like this were available for all transtypes, we could verify most activity records using Db Fit with ease.

!|Query|!- SELECT ACCTNBR, TRANSNBR, OPERID, TRANSTYPE,            
  SUBSTR(ACTMISC,1,1)AS OLDPROMSTS,
  SUBSTR(ACTMISC,2,1)AS NEWPROMSTS,
  SUBSTR(ACTMISC,3,1)AS OLDTELESTS,
  SUBSTR(ACTMISC,4,1)AS NEWTELESTS,
  SUBSTR(ACTMISC,5,1)AS OLDSWEEPSTS,
  SUBSTR(ACTMISC,6,1)AS NEWSWEEPSTS,
  SUBSTR(ACTMISC,7,1)AS OLDENVIRSTS,
  SUBSTR(ACTMISC,8,1)AS NEWENVIRSTS,
  SUBSTR(ACTMISC,9,1)AS OLDCONTSTS,
  SUBSTR(ACTMISC,10,1)AS NEWCONTSTS
          FROM CLTLIBd6x.XACTIVITY0 WHERE TRANSTYPE = 31
         AND ACCTNBR = 27062579
         AND TRANSNBR > 112108000000001                                  -!|
